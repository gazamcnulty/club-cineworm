About Club Cineworm

Club Identity

Club Cineworm is a welcoming place for people to talk about movies and books. Our main focus is adaptations ; popular books which are then made into films. This allows anyone to take part, if you just read the book or saw the movie , or if you have a perspective on both which can be fun to compare.

Club History

Club Cineworm began in 2018 , from a likeminded group of people on an irish movie forum , who all happended to live in Dublin. Since then the group has grown in size and popularity. The covid pandemic shifted the club into a zoom based format, but ultimately many of us were grateful to have a regular outlet to speak to friendly members about films and books. After the lockdowns ended it was decided to keep zoom calls as a meetup method every now and then, as well as our usual coffee and cinema meet ups.

Club Beliefs and Values

  • Being welcoming to all sorts of people
  • Everyone should be given a space to share their thoughts on a film or book
  • We believe that art only exists in how it is interpreted by the individual who beholds it
  • New artistic experiences should be saught out, engaged with and discussed to reach its full potential

Club Goals

  • Providing a welcoming space for anyone and everyone
  • Introducing people to new film and literature experiences
  • Engaging in discussions about all sorts of topics
  • Encouraging members to engage in similar activities and contribute to the discourse
  • Hopefully make aquantainces and friends along the way
